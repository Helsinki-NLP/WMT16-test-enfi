Aftonbladet: Hungarian police use tear gas against refugees
Hungarian police has used tear gas against hundreds of refugees.
According to the news agency AP, the incident took place on Hungary's border with Serbia, where many refugees attempt to cross to Europe.
Sky News says refugees attempted to break through a fence.
Refugees, many of which were women and children, took flight in a panic.
According to local media, Hungarian police also allegedly used water cannons against people.
