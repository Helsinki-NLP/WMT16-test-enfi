First company-run reception centre opens in Espoo
The first company-run reception centre for adult asylum seekers opens in Otaniemi district of Espoo.
The reception centre is ready to welcome the first hundred occupants already today from 6 PM onwards.
The space may increase within a week to accommodate a maximum of 300 to 400 people.
The Finnish Immigration Service sets up the reception centre on the facilities of a former emergency services college.
A company specialising in healthcare and medical services production, Luona Oy, will run the operations.
The City of Espoo, the Finnish Immigration Service and Luona Oy agree together for instance on the security arrangements of the centre.
