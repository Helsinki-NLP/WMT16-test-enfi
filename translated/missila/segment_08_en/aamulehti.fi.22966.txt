A 9-year-old boy missing at Tampere was found
The police organised a search at Tampere on Tuesday evening because a 9-year old boy ran away.
The boy started off from western Tampere with a friend, and a missing person notice spread in social media.
According to police situation centre, the boy has been contacted and there is no danger to his life or health.
Around midnight, the father of the boy informed Ilta-Sanomat that the boy has been found.
He is fine, and now we begin to settle the situation.
The boy was found near home, the father said.
