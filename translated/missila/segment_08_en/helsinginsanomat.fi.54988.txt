A shop was robbed at gunpoint in Oulu
A shop was robbed on Wednesday morning in Kello district of Oulu.
According to the police, the robbed wore dark clothes and had covered his face with a balaclava.
The man is of average height and weight, and about 30 years old.
According to the police, the man had threatened the shop staff with a hand gun and managed to take a small sum of money.
The matter is being investigated as an aggravated robbery.
